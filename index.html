Selected Projects

Branding / Figma / UI UX / Agile / Frontend

BB Spa is a baby wellness experience developed for StartPhysio. I was Team Lead, UX/UI Designer, and Frontend Developer. Using Agile methodology (discover, define, ideate, implement), we created personas, empathy maps, problem statements, flowcharts, and a sitemap. The branding was built from scratch, including logo, tagline, visual identity, and a playful mascot named Goutty to create a warm and welcoming atmosphere. I led the design process and frontend implementation, ensuring a seamless and engaging user experience.

Figma / Illustrator / Agile / HTML & CSS

Flora is a simplified e-commerce website designed with a user-friendly experience at its core. The project centers around "Flora," a fictional brand created to give context and personality to the design. The website was prototyped in Figma and implemented using HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap, with an emphasis on minimalism and usability.

Figma / Illustrator / Agile / HTML & CSS / Javascript

ShapeUp is an exclusive gym brand focused on delivering personalized experiences and individual guidance, distinguishing itself from traditional gyms. This project was developed as part of the Web Programming and Design Techniques course at CESAE. I was responsible for the design, project management (using Agile methodology), and also contributed to programming, ensuring a smooth and efficient development process.

Illustrator / Photoshop / Figma

The visual identity for MP was designed to balance creativity and reliability, blending adaptability with a strong corporate foundation. I developed the logo and its applications across various platforms, from stationery like notebooks and stamps to digital assets such as social media, email signatures, and the website. The modular and versatile design ensures the logo seamlessly adapts to diverse materials and contexts, bringing its dynamic nature to life through thoughtful animations.

Illustrator / InDesign / Figma

In Maputo, this bank merger was portrayed as a smooth handover of trust and quality. Billboards and public ads featured the image of a torch being passed, symbolizing continuity. The campaign reassured customers that despite the change in ownership, they could still count on the same reliable service and care.

Photoshop / Illustrator / Illustration / Collage

As soon as I moved to Lisbon in 2016 I started a very strong relationship with the city's musical culture. I organized concerts, got involved in event production and developed printed and digital graphic materials (posters, flyers, banners, among others) with the aim of promoting these same events. My name is Sofia Santos, but you can call me Shia. I’m a UX/UI Designer who recently became a Frontend Developer, which enables me to follow each project from start to finish—designing, building, and refining it. This gives me the opportunity to create solutions that are visually appealing, meaningful, and functional.
I’m always working to improve my communication and to be collaborative and proactive, bringing enthusiasm, curiosity, and a touch of humour to everything I do.

Committed to continuous learning, I embrace mistakes as opportunities for growth and stay open to new discoveries along the way.
I also have a deep curiosity about other cultures and am always looking to learn new skills—whether it's mastering a design tool, exploring a coding technique, or even how to make the perfect negroni.
