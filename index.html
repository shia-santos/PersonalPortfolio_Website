My name is Sofia Santos, but you can call me Shia. I’m a UX/UI Designer who recently became a Frontend Developer, which enables me to follow each project from start to finish—designing, building, and refining it. This gives me the opportunity to create solutions that are visually appealing, meaningful, and functional.
I’m always working to improve my communication and to be collaborative and proactive, bringing enthusiasm, curiosity, and a touch of humour to everything I do.

Committed to continuous learning, I embrace mistakes as opportunities for growth and stay open to new discoveries along the way.
I also have a deep curiosity about other cultures and am always looking to learn new skills—whether it's mastering a design tool, exploring a coding technique, or even how to make the perfect negroni.
